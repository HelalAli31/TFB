Our Story

Founded with a passion for quality and innovation, The Fog Bank has grown from a small local shop to a trusted name in the vaping community. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional vaping products that meet the highest standards of safety and performance.

Today, we continue to uphold these values as we expand our selection and reach more customers who share our enthusiasm for premium vaping experiences.

We currently operate two physical stores located in Ibillin and Kfar Yassif, where our knowledgeable staff is ready to help you find the perfect product.
